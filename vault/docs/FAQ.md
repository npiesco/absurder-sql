# VaultApp FAQ

## Is my data stored in the cloud?
No. VaultApp is local-first and keeps vault data on-device unless you export and move backups yourself.

## What happens if I forget my master password?
Vault data is encrypted. Without the master password, the vault cannot be decrypted.

## Does VaultApp support biometric unlock?
Yes. Biometric unlock can be enabled in Settings on supported devices.

## Can I use VaultApp offline?
Yes. Core credential access and editing work offline.

## How do I move to a new device?
Export a backup from the old device, transfer it securely, then import it on the new device.

## Are one-time codes supported?
Yes. VaultApp supports TOTP secrets and rotating 6-digit codes.

## Does VaultApp auto-lock?
Yes. Auto-lock timeout is configurable in Settings.

## Can I clear copied passwords automatically?
Yes. Clipboard auto-clear timeout can be configured in Settings.
